Ultra-Modern Theatre Complex

Laminar-flow theatres for advanced laparoscopic & open surgery

Ultra-Modern Theatre Complex

Laminar airflow keeps the surgical field sterile through every operation — the standard used in top metro hospitals. Advanced laparoscopic and open surgeries happen here with modern anaesthesia workstations.

Endoscopy Unit

Diagnostic & therapeutic endoscopy, same-day reporting

Endoscopy Unit

A camera-guided look inside the food pipe, stomach and colon — a short daycare procedure with same-day reporting, for everything from persistent acidity to preventive screening.

ICU & NICU

Round-the-clock intensive care for adults and newborns

ICU & NICU

Monitored critical-care beds for adults and a dedicated newborn ICU — specialist teams and nursing awake round the clock, every day of the year.

Laboratory & Diagnostics

Clinical lab, radiodiagnosis & fetal ultrasound

Laboratory & Diagnostics

Clinical lab, X-ray, ultrasound and fetal scanning under one roof — so your tests, reports and doctor’s review can happen in a single visit.

24×7 Pharmacy

In-house pharmacy, stocked and open at all hours

24×7 Pharmacy

Stocked and open at all hours — including the emergency medicine you need at 3 AM. You never leave with a prescription unfilled.

Physiotherapy
